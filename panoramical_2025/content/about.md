## About
Panoramical (2015) is a **collection of playful interactive landscapes**: part art installation, part musical instrument.  
With a mixer board controller to manipulate visuals and sounds and no predefined goals, players find beautiful combinations of shapes and soundscapes, setting the pace of their own audiovisual journey.  
The result is an immersive experience for players and spectators. 

Panoramical been featured in museums and exhibits worldwide, and released as a [downloadable PC game](https://finji.itch.io/panoramical).

* **an interactive audiovisual experience projected on a screen and played with a controller.**
* Duration: Infinite play, regular playthrough 5-20 minutes
* Requirements: Projector, PC Computer, [Korg nanoKONTROL controller](https://reverb.com/p/korg-nanokontrol2-slimeline-usb-midi-control-surface), Headphones

## Artists

**Fernando Ramallo** is a Berlin-based multidisciplinary artist from Argentina. He works with custom software to create immersive computer-based art. His playful interactive work sparks wonder and curiosity and has been on display at 45+ museums and festivals worlwide, incl. SF MoMA, SIGGRAPH and Sonar Festival.

**David Kanaga** is a composer from California. Alongside game soundtracks, his works include the dog opera "Oikospiel" (2017), built with tools by Fernando Ramallo, and the unabridged MIDI deconstruction of Wagner's opera Die Walküre, "Soft Valkyrie"  (2022), narrated by Stephen Fry, commissioned by Holland Festival, Staatsoper Hanover + Dutch National Opera.
