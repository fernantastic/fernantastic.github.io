About

Panoramical (2015) is a collection of playful interactive landscapes: part art installation, part musical instrument   With a mixer board controller to manipulate visuals and sounds and no predefined goals, players find beautiful combinations of shapes and soundscapes, setting the pace of their own audiovisual journey   The result is an immersive experience for players and spectators  

Panoramical been featured in museums and exhibits worldwide, and released as a downloadable PC game.

  • an interactive audiovisual experience projected on a screen and played with a mixer-style controller.
  • Duration: Infinite play, regular playthrough 5-20 minutes
  • Requirements: Projector, PC Computer, Korg nanoKONTROL controller, Headphones

Artists

Fernando Ramallo is a Berlin-based multidisciplinary artist from Argentina. He works with custom software to create immersive computer-based art. His playful interactive work sparks wonder and curiosity and has been on display at museums and festivals worlwide, including SF MoMA, SIGGRAPH and BOZAR.

David Kanaga is a composer from California  Alongside game soundtracks, his works include the dog opera “Oikospiel” (2017), built with tools by Fernando Ramallo, and the unabridged MIDI deconstruction of Wagner’s opera Die Walküre, “Soft Valkyrie” (2022), narrated by Stephen Fry, commissioned by Holland Festival, Staatsoper Hanover + Dutch National Opera.
