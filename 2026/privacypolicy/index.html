Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Tiny Walk apps and services handle information.

Summary

Tiny Walk apps and services do not collect, transmit, store, or share personal information unless explicitly stated otherwise in a product-specific notice.

Our apps and services are designed to operate without accounts, analytics, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking unless clearly disclosed for a specific product.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal data or usage data.

Specifically, we do not:

  • create user accounts
  • collect names, email addresses, contact details, or payment information
  • track app usage or user behavior
  • use analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services
  • upload user content to our servers
  • share data with third parties

Permissions

Some apps may request device permissions only when needed to provide core functionality.

When a permission is requested, any related processing is intended to happen locally on your device unless a product-specific notice says otherwise.

On-Device Storage

Our apps may store a small amount of configuration locally on your device using standard platform storage. This can include items such as:

  • window size and position
  • app preferences
  • feature settings
  • whether introductory UI has already been shown

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us.

Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Our apps and services do not include third-party analytics, advertising, or data-broker integrations unless clearly disclosed for a specific product.

Data Retention and Deletion

Because we do not collect personal data, we do not retain personal data on our servers.

If you want to remove locally stored app preferences from your device, you can delete the app and its local data from your device.

Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or from any users.

Changes to This Policy

If our privacy practices change in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated here.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the app developer through the relevant support channel listed for the app or service.
