Projector Fix

Projector Fix helps you correct the perspective of your display going into a projector.

  • Available now for macOS, download it now in the App Store.

How To Use:

  1. Drag the Output Window to your secondary display and make it fullscreen.

  2. In the Settings, choose your input content and drag the image corners to adjust the image until it looks correct on the wall.
